About Us

The Bardbarians are an ultimate frisbee team that plays in the NYC ultimate frisbee A league. The Bardbarians also play in tournaments throughout the tri-state area the biggest which have been the Youth Ultimate League of Arlington tournament in Virginia and the New York State Championship in Albany. From 2019-2021, the Brdbarians have taken home two city championships and one state championship with an overall record of 33-1.

Our Mission

Our mission is to hold our place as the strongest ultimate frisbee program in New York State while while making sure the student in student-athlete stays as important as it should be.

How To Join

The Bardbarians are the ultimate frisbee team for Bard High School Early College Manhattan and our players are currently students there. If you attend BHSEC Manhattan or are applying/accepted student you can sign up for our mailing list below or at the fall club fair

Additional Information

The Bardbarians currently practice year round on Mondays, Tuesdays, and Thursdays at parks right near our school. We try to attend as many tournaments as possible which occur on the weekends in the fall and in the spring.
